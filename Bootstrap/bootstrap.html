Tampa Bay AutoX

Welcome to join us! Our next event is in Sunday, Oct 1 on Brooksville Regional Airport.

Autocross is a motorsport that focuses on driver skill. Events are held on paved lots using traffic cones to define a course that is run one car at a time for best time. Most folks compete in daily driven street cars. Autocross is considered the safest of all motorsports. It is inexpensive and fun!
